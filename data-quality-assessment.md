Data Quality Assessment

Introduction

This project demonstrates techniques used to evaluate, validate, and improve data quality for reliable analytics and reporting.

Objectives

- Assess data accuracy and completeness.
- Identify inconsistencies and errors.
- Improve data reliability.
- Support trustworthy business insights.

Skills Demonstrated

- Data Validation
- Data Cleaning
- Quality Assessment
- Reporting
- Analytical Problem Solving

Business Impact

High-quality data improves decision-making, reporting accuracy, and overall organizational efficiency.

Conclusion

Data quality assessment is a critical step in ensuring that analytical insights are accurate, reliable, and actionable.
